STORY

It all started when Jessie Gamulo was frustrated by the lack of stylish, comfortable, and sustainable t-shirts on the market. After months of research and design, Jessie's T-shirt was born. Every design is crafted with care, using only the best materials so you can wear it confidently, day after day.

Jessie Gamulo
